About us

Founded in 2009, Suzhou Hiker Technology Co., Ltd is an innovative venture which is dedicated in tourism services based on mobile internet technology. It is a pioneer in the intelligent tourism industry in China. The company has developed and published three mobile internet applications (APPs) , which are: “One by one”, “Haiyou” and “Go easy“.
APP “Go easy” – Mobile ecommerce platform for Chinese people travel abroad App “Go easy”was first lunched in October 2014. It is a dedicated mobile ecommerce platform for Chinese people travel abroad. The platform support on-line booking of varies global local commodities with off-line delivery to the airport before the return flight, at the meantime, it also provide other on-line booking services including 4G Mobile Wi-Fi, car rent, transportation ticket and day-tour, etc. This on-line to off-line solution allows Chinese overseas tourists to enjoy easier travel experience including communication, shopping and site-seeing; which is also a direct and efficient bridge in between Chinese tourists and overseas merchants.
The company has owned 10 software copyright and 3 approved international IPs, with other 51 IPs under application. The company grows with the strong support from Chinese governments, it has won certificates such as “National Hi-tech Enterprises” and “Tech pioneer Enterprises in Suzhou”.
